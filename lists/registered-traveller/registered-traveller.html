Registered Traveller - summary
    Declaration Registered Traveller - declaration

    I confirm that:

    • I have read, understood and accept the Terms and conditions and the Privacy Policy of the Registered Traveller Service
    • I will tell Border Force if my circumstances change, or if any new information relevant to my application becomes available
    • The information supplied is complete, true and correct to the best of my knowledge

    I understand that:

    • My membership of the service is conditional upon acceptance by Border Force and lasts for 12 months from the date I am provisionally accepted
    • My details may in certain circumstances be passed to fraud prevention agencies to prevent and detect fraud and money laundering
    • Other agencies may provide Border Force with information about me
    • A £70 payment is required to apply to join the Registered Traveller Service. The payment includes a non-refundable £20 administration fee. The remaining £50 is for 12 months membership of Registered Traveller should the application be successful
